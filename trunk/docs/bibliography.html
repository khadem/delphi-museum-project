Delphi: Bibliography

This document lists selected papers, articles, web pages and other bibiliographic references related to the Delphi project, an open source software system that helps open up access via the web to collections of artifacts held by museums, historical societies, archeological repositories and similar heritage institutions.

Black, Michael, "From no access to open access in two years - experiences and lessons learned at the Phoebe A. Hearst Museum of Anthropology," Statens Senter for Arkiv, Bibliotek og Museum (ABM) - Konferansen 2008 - Kulturarven til Alle [Government Center for Archives, Libraries and Museums (ABM) - Conference 2008 - Culture for All], November 10, 2008 [presentation slides]
http://www.abm-utvikling.no/digitalt-abm/abm-konferansen-2008/black.pdf

Schmitz, Patrick, "Delphi project exposes PAHMA's collections to the world," Berkeley Computing & Communications, September 2, 2008 (Fall 2008 edition) [newsletter article]
http://inews.berkeley.edu/articles/Fall2008/delphi

Schmitz, Patrick and Black, Michael, "The Delphi Toolkit: Enabling Semantic Search for Museum Collections," Museums and the Web 2008: The International Conference for Culture and Heritage On-Line, April 9-12, 2008 [conference paper]
http://www.archimuse.com/mw2008/papers/schmitz/schmitz.html

Amuzinskaya, Olga; Hilgert, Adrienne; Lesser, John; Schmitz, Patrick and Yu, Gerald, "Delphi: An online museum collection browser," School of Information, University of California, Berkeley, May 3, 2007 [graduate school project paper]
http://www.ischool.berkeley.edu/files/DelphiFinalReportLinked.pdf
